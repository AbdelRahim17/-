Blender Swiss Roll Trend

Blender Swiss Roll is a unique and trendy recipe made from flour, eggs, milk, sugar, coffee, cocoa, and baking powder. It is prepared as a rolled cake filled with cream and topped with chocolate for a delicious treat.

Ingredients:

  1. 250g whipped cream
  2. 200g powdered sugar
  3. 200g milk
  4. 150g all-purpose flour
  5. 55g raw cocoa powder
  6. 150g oil
  7. 2 eggs (room temperature)
  8. 10g baking powder
  9. 5g coffee
  10. A pinch of salt

Preparation Method

In a blender, add the milk and powdered sugar, blending well until the sugar dissolves completely. Add the eggs and oil, then blend again until the mixture is smooth and well combined. In a separate bowl, mix the coffee, salt, and cocoa powder until fully incorporated. Sift in the flour and baking powder, then mix well. Gradually add the liquid mixture to the dry ingredients while blending until a smooth cake batter forms. Pour the batter into a baking tray lined with parchment paper and bake in a preheated oven at 180Â°C for 25 minutes. Once baked, let the cake cool completely, then spread whipped cream evenly over it. Roll the cake carefully into a log shape, then drizzle with chocolate sauce. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours before serving. Enjoy your delicious Swiss Roll!
