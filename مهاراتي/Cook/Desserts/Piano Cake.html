Turkish Piano Cake

Turkish Piano Cake is a special dessert made in an easy two-layer process. The first layer consists of a soft, spongy chocolate cake, while the second layer is a creamy milk-based topping. The cake is beautifully decorated with cocoa powder and chocolate sticks to resemble piano keys.

Ingredients:

  1. 1 egg
  2. 1 cup flour
  3. ½ cup sugar
  4. ½ cup vegetable oil
  5. ½ cup liquid milk
  6. ¼ cup cocoa powder
  7. 1 tbsp baking powder
  8. 1 tsp vanilla

    9. For the second layer:

  9. 2.5 cups liquid milk
  10. 1 cup whipping cream
  11. 5 tbsp sugar
  12. 2 tbsp flour
  13. 1 tbsp cornstarch

    14. For decoration:

  14. 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  15. Chocolate sticks

Preparation Method

In a bowl, beat the egg with vanilla and sugar until well combined and the sugar dissolves. Add vegetable oil and milk, mixing well. Gradually add cocoa powder, baking powder, and flour while stirring continuously. Pour the batter into a baking dish and bake in a preheated oven at 180°C for 30 minutes. Once baked, remove from the oven and set aside to cool.

Preparing the second layer:
In a saucepan over medium heat, add the milk, sugar, cornstarch, and flour. Stir well until combined. 7. Let the mixture cook for about 5 minutes after boiling, stirring continuously until it thickens. 8. Add the whipping cream and mix well. 9. Pour the prepared mixture over the cooled cake. 10. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours until the second layer sets. 11. Dust the surface with cocoa powder and arrange chocolate sticks on top to resemble piano keys. Serve and enjoy!
