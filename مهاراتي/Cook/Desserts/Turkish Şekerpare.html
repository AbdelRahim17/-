Turkish Şekerpare

Turkish Şekerpare is a delicious traditional Turkish dessert made from a dough of flour, semolina, powdered sugar, eggs, butter, vanilla, baking powder, and nuts. It is baked until golden and soaked in syrup for a rich, sweet flavor.

Ingredients:

  1. 3 cups flour
  2. 1 cup syrup (simple syrup)
  3. 250g butter or margarine
  4. 2 eggs
  5. ½ cup semolina
  6. ½ cup powdered sugar
  7. 5g vanilla
  8. 10g baking powder

    9. For the topping:

  9. 1 egg yolk
  10. 1 tsp grape extract (optional)
  11. 2 tbsp chopped nuts

Preparation Method

In a bowl, mix all ingredients except flour and baking powder. Gradually add the baking powder and flour while kneading until a smooth, moldable dough forms. Divide the dough into equal-sized balls and place them on a baking tray lined with parchment paper. Mix the egg yolk with grape extract and brush the dough balls with the mixture. Use a fork to create lines on top, then sprinkle with chopped nuts. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C for 30 minutes until golden brown. Remove from the oven, let cool for 2 minutes, then pour the cold syrup over them. Let the pastries soak for 10 minutes, then drain any excess syrup. Serve and enjoy!
