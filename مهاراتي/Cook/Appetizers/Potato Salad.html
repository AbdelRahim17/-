Potato Salad

Italian Potato Salad with Salami is a unique and flavorful dish that is easy and quick to prepare. It consists of boiled, diced potatoes mixed with parsley, onion, and salami slices, seasoned with salt, pepper, and olive oil for a delicious and refreshing taste.

Ingredients:

  1. 2 large potatoes, peeled and cut into medium cubes
  2. 6 slices of salami, chopped
  3. 1 onion, finely chopped
  4. 3 tbsp olive oil
  5. 2 tbsp finely chopped parsley
  6. ¼ tsp salt
  7. ¼ tsp black pepper

Preparation Method

In a pot over medium heat, bring hot water to a boil and add the diced potatoes. Let them cook for about 15 minutes until tender but still firm. Drain and transfer to a serving bowl. Add the chopped salami, onion, and parsley to the potatoes. Sprinkle with salt and black pepper, then drizzle with olive oil. Toss everything together gently and serve. Enjoy!
