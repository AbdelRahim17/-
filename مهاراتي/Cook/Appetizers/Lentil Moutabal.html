Lentil Moutabal

Lentil Moutabal is a delicious and flavorful appetizer that is easy to prepare. It is made by boiling lentils
with water and cumin, then draining and blending them with tahini, lemon juice, mashed garlic, and salt.

Ingredients:

  1. 300g Brown Lentils
  2. 1.5 cups Tahini
  3. ¾ cup Lemon Juice
  4. 6 cloves Garlic, mashed
  5. 2 tbsp Olive Oil & Chopped Parsley
  6. ¾ tbsp Salt
  7. 1 tsp Cumin

Preparation Method

Wash the lentils and place them in a pot with water over medium heat. Add cumin, stir, and let the mixture boil. After boiling, let it cook for 10 minutes, then drain the lentils and allow them to cool. Once cooled, transfer the lentils to a food processor and add tahini, lemon juice, mashed garlic, and salt. Blend well until you get a thick and smooth consistency. Pour into a serving dish, garnish with olive oil and parsley, and enjoy!
