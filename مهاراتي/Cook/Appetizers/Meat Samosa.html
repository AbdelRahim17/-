Meat Samosa

Fattet Meat Samosa is a unique and delicious dish that is easy and quick to prepare. It consists of crispy fried meat samosas served with a flavorful sauce made from yogurt, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and salt, making it a delightful and appetizing meal.

Ingredients:

  1. 12 pieces of meat samosa
  2. 2.5 cups yogurt
  3. ¼ cup tahini
  4. ¼ cup lemon juice
  5. 2 cloves garlic, mashed
  6. 3 tbsp melted butter
  7. ½ tsp salt

Preparation Method

Arrange the samosas on a baking tray lined with parchment paper, brush the tops with melted butter, and bake at 180°C for 15 minutes until golden and crispy. In a bowl, mix yogurt, tahini, lemon juice, mashed garlic, and salt until well combined. Spread a portion of the sauce on a serving dish, then layer the crispy samosas on top. Pour the remaining sauce over them. Garnish with nuts, pomegranate seeds, and chopped parsley. Serve and enjoy!
