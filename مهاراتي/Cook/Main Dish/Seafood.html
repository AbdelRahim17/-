Seafood Tagine with Squid and Shrimp

A delicious and flavorful seafood tagine prepared easily with shrimp, squid, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and bell peppers, all cooked together for a rich and aromatic dish.

Ingredients:

  1. 1 kg peeled shrimp
  2. ½ kg squid, sliced
  3. 2 tbsp olive oil
  4. 1 large onion, finely chopped
  5. 8 garlic cloves, minced
  6. 1 red chili pepper, chopped
  7. 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  8. 1.5 bunches fresh coriander, chopped
  9. 2 tomatoes, diced
  10. 1 tbsp tomato paste
  11. ½ tsp salt
  12. ½ tsp black pepper

Preparation Method

In a pan over medium heat, sauté the shrimp and squid with olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and black pepper until slightly golden. Remove and set aside. In the same pan, add 2 tbsp olive oil, diced tomatoes, chopped onion, red chili, green bell pepper, and tomato paste. Stir well and let cook for 15 minutes until the vegetables soften. Add the chopped fresh coriander and stir for 2 minutes. In a tagine or ovenproof dish, layer half of the cooked vegetable mixture at the bottom. Add the sautéed shrimp and squid, then top with the remaining vegetable mixture. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C for 15 minutes until the flavors blend and the sauce thickens. Serve hot and enjoy!
