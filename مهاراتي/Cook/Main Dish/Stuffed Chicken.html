Stuffed Chicken with Grape Leaves

A delicious oven-roasted chicken stuffed with rolled grape leaves filled with rice, vegetables, lemon juice, and olive oil.

Ingredients:

    For the Chicken:
  1. 1 whole chicken (1.4 kg)
    2. For the Chicken Marinade:
  2. 4 tbsp olive oil
  3. 2 tbsp white vinegar
  4. 1 tbsp red pepper paste
  5. 1 tbsp tomato paste
  6. 4 garlic cloves, minced
  7. 1 tsp salt
  8. ½ tsp black pepper
    9. For the Grape Leaves Stuffing:
  9. 600 g fresh grape leaves
  10. 1.5 cups rice, washed and soaked
  11. 1 onion, finely chopped
  12. ½ bunch parsley, finely chopped
  13. ¼ bunch fresh mint leaves, chopped
  14. 4 tbsp pomegranate molasses
  15. 4 tbsp lemon juice
  16. 4 tbsp olive oil
  17. 1 tbsp red pepper paste
  18. ¾ tsp salt
  19. ½ tsp black pepper

Preparation Method

Prepare the Grape Leaves:
Boil water in a pot over medium heat.
Add the grape leaves and blanch for 3 minutes until they soften and turn from bright green to a yellowish-green.
Drain and set aside.
Prepare the Stuffing:
In a bowl, mix the soaked rice with all the stuffing ingredients.
Combine well to allow the flavors to blend.
Roll the grape leaves into small stuffed rolls and set aside.
Prepare the Chicken:
In a separate bowl, mix all the marinade ingredients.
Rub the marinade all over the chicken, making sure to coat it well.
Stuff the Chicken:
Fill the chicken cavity with the prepared stuffed grape leaves.
Place the remaining stuffed grape leaves around the chicken in the baking dish.
Bake the Chicken:
Pour 2 cups of hot water into the dish, season with a little salt and black pepper.
Cover the dish with aluminum foil.
Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C for 1.5 hours until fully cooked.
Serve:
Remove from the oven, transfer to a serving dish, and serve hot.
