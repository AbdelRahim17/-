Potato Gratin with Béchamel

A delicious oven-baked dish made with layers of mashed potatoes, seasoned ground meat with onions, garlic, and parsley, topped with creamy béchamel sauce and melted mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients:

  1. 2 kg potatoes, peeled and sliced
  2. ¾ tsp salt
  3. ½ tsp black pepper
    4. For the Meat Layer:
  4. 1 kg ground beef
  5. 1 large onion, finely chopped
  6. 2 garlic cloves, minced
  7. ½ bunch parsley, chopped
  8. ¾ tsp salt
  9. ½ tsp black pepper
    10. For the Béchamel Sauce:
  10. 5 cups whole milk
  11. 4 tbsp butter
  12. 6 tbsp flour
  13. ½ tsp salt
  14. A pinch of nutmeg
  15. A pinch of black pepper
    16. For Topping:
  16. 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Preparation Method

Boil the Potatoes: Place the sliced potatoes in a pot of salted water over medium heat and boil for 30 minutes until fully cooked. Drain and mash until smooth.
Prepare the Meat Layer: In a pan over medium heat, add vegetable oil, then sauté the onions and garlic for 5 minutes until softened. Add the ground beef, salt, black pepper, and parsley, stirring well. Cook on low heat for 15 minutes until fully done.
Season the Mashed Potatoes: Mix the mashed potatoes with a little salt and black pepper until well combined.
Prepare the Béchamel Sauce: In a pot over medium heat, melt the butter, then add the flour and stir for 1 minute. Gradually add the milk while stirring continuously to prevent lumps. Add salt, black pepper, and nutmeg, then let it simmer for 2 minutes until thickened. Remove from heat.
Assemble the Dish:
Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
Spread the mashed potatoes evenly in the tray.
Add the cooked meat layer on top.
Pour the béchamel sauce over the meat.
Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
Bake: Preheat the oven to 180°C and bake for 17 minutes until the cheese melts and turns golden. Serve: Remove from the oven, slice, and serve hot. Enjoy
