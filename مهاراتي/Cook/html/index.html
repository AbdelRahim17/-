Appetizers

Lentil Moutabal

Lentil Moutabal is a delicious and flavorful appetizer that is easy to prepare. It is made by boiling lentils with water and cumin, then draining and blending them with tahini, lemon juice, mashed garlic, and salt.

Meat Samosa

Fattet Meat Samosa is a unique and delicious dish that is easy and quick to prepare. It consists of crispy fried meat samosas topped with a sauce made from yogurt, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and salt, creating a flavorful and appetizing meal.

Potato Salad

Italian Potato Salad with Salami is a unique and flavorful dish that is easy and quick to prepare. It consists of boiled, diced potatoes mixed with parsley, onion, and salami slices, seasoned with salt, pepper, and olive oil for a delicious and refreshing taste.

Desserts

Blender Swiss Roll Trend

Blender Swiss Roll is a unique and trendy recipe made from flour, eggs, milk, sugar, coffee, cocoa, and baking powder. It is prepared as a rolled cake filled with cream and topped with chocolate for a delicious treat.

Turkish Şekerpare Dessert

Turkish Şekerpare is a delicious traditional Turkish dessert made from a dough of flour, semolina, powdered sugar, eggs, butter, vanilla, baking powder, and nuts. It is baked until golden and soaked in syrup for a rich, sweet flavor.

Turkish Piano Cake

Turkish Piano Cake is a special dessert made in an easy two-layer process. The first layer consists of a soft, spongy chocolate cake, while the second layer is a creamy milk-based topping. The cake is beautifully decorated with cocoa powder and chocolate sticks to resemble piano keys.

Main Dish

Seafood Tagine with Squid and Shrimp

A delicious and flavorful seafood tagine prepared easily with shrimp, squid, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and bell peppers, all cooked together for a rich and aromatic dish.

Potato Gratin with Béchamel

A delicious oven-baked dish made with layers of mashed potatoes, seasoned ground meat with onions, garlic, and parsley, topped with creamy béchamel sauce and melted mozzarella cheese.

Stuffed Chicken with Grape Leaves

A delicious oven-roasted chicken stuffed with rolled grape leaves filled with rice, vegetables, lemon juice, and olive oil.
